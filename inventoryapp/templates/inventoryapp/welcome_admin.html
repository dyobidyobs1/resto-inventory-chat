About Us

Tomguts Food Station is a food business located at Mandaluyong City, Metro Manila and has 9 branches all over Luzon. Products offered are mainly Crispy Pata and Crispy Ulo. It all happened on one night when the business owners were having a drink and thought of making a pulutan food business and then planned everything out. They are famously known for having a Buy 1 Take 1 Crispy Pata and Crispy Ulo around the city.
